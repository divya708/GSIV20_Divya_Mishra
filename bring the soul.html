Bring The Soul:The Movie Rating:90%

Aug 07, 2019| 2hr 10min| Park Jun-soo

Cast: Kim Nam-joon, Kim Seok-jin, Min Yoon-gi

Description: Following their landmark “Love Yourself” tour, BTS triumphantly returns to cinema screens in Bring the Soul: The Movie. Shining brighter than any light on the stage, now the group invite us behind the spotlight. On the day following the final concert of their Europe tour, on a rooftop in Paris, BTS tells their very own stories from experiencing new cities to performing in front of thousands of ARMY across the globe. A glimpse into BTS’ world away from the stage, featuring intimate group discussions alongside spectacular concert performances from the tour, this is a cinema event not to be missed. The journey of BTS continues.
